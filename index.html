Recognize The Potential

Approximately 300 million people migrate each year from all over the world, which generates over $140 billion in revenue for the immigration industry.

Keeping those numbers in mind, we are going to focus on 3 major points

Money Remittance and Distribution

Immi-coin holds a licensed Exchange and wallet system that will enable immigrants to send money to their relatives all over the world in minimum effort and cost using our issued pre-paid card or digital wallet, making the unbanked immigrants bankable. More than that, businesses will be able to use our services for monthly salary distribution, making the process care-free and much easier.

Regulated Immigration

As a middleman, Immi-Coin will connect service providers with individuals through an online marketplace, eliminating insecurity, lack of service, fraud, and more that arise due to the lack of regulations. With our platform, which is built on smart contracts and tokenized payments, all of these problems will be solved.

Bringing Immigration To The Clouds

By utilizing Immi-coin’s NFT technology immigrants could upload all relevant documents to our application to create an electronic file containing all the information at one time and provide this file directly to service providers or any organization that requires these sorts of information.
